Experience the latest innovations in design, speed, and functionality

Features

  • Stunning 6.5-inch OLED display
  • Advanced A18 Bionic Chip
  • 5G Connectivity
  • Pro Camera System with Night Mode
  • All-day battery life
  • Face ID and Fingerprint Unlock

Camera:

  1. Periscope Telephoto Lens - Provides up to 10x optical zoom, enabling high-quality close-ups from far distances.

  2. Astrophotography Mode - Specialized setting for capturing the night sky, stars, and low-light landscapes with enhanced clarity.

  3. Cinematic Mode 2.0 - Allows for even smoother focus transitions and manual control over focal points, ideal for professional-grade video recording.

  4. Macro Photography Enhancements - Improved ultra-wide lens with AI detail enhancement for capturing intricate close-up shots.

  5. Dual Selfie Camera System - Features an ultra-wide lens for group selfies and enhanced portrait effects for a professional look on the front-facing camera.

A18 Bionic Chip

  1. Next-Gen CPU & GPU Performance: Boosts speed and responsiveness, with up to 20% faster processing power for intensive apps, gaming, and multitasking.

  2. Energy-Efficient 3nm Architecture: Built on a 3nm process, offering longer battery life and reduced heat generation, allowing for sustained high performance.

  3. Neural Engine with Faster AI Processing: Capable of trillions of operations per second, accelerating machine learning tasks, from image processing to real-time translation and on-device AI.

  4. Enhanced Graphics for AR & VR: GPU optimized for smooth, immersive augmented reality and virtual reality experiences with richer graphics and higher frame rates.

  5. Upgraded Security: Provides enhanced protection for sensitive data, like Face ID and Apple Pay, with advanced encryption for top-tier security.
